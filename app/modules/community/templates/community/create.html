Tips for Creating a Community
What is a Community?

A community is a space that groups related datasets under a common theme or institution.

Choosing a Name
  • Use clear, descriptive names
  • Avoid abbreviations unless widely known
  • Keep it concise but informative
Writing a Description
  • Explain the community's purpose and scope
  • Mention target audience or research areas
  • Describe what types of datasets belong here
Community Logo
  • Square images work best (e.g., 500x500px)
  • Use high contrast for visibility
  • Simple designs are more recognizable
Note: You will automatically become a curator of this community and can manage dataset submissions.
